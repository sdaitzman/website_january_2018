Sam loves engineering and design, writing, research and projects across disciplines. She is a recent graduate of NuVu Studio, an engineering, design and innovation school in Cambridge, Massachusetts. She studies online and at Harvard. She writes poetry, prose, lots of code and sometimes fiction. She tends to a garden featuring native plants and rare cacti. She takes lots of photos. She is currently working at the Berkman Klein Center for Internet and Society at Harvard University. Sam has experience with 3D design and printing tech, mechanical engineering, robotics, teaching, Arduino/C, web design and development, hardware engineering and design, video and photography, writing, research, tech evangelism, software development and composing self-referential paragraphs in the third person. Sam is transgender and prefers she/her pronouns.
